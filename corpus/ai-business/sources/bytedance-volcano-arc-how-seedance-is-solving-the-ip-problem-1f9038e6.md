# "ByteDance Volcano Arc: How Seedance Is Solving the IP Problem for AI Video"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.mindstudio.ai/blog/bytedance-volcano-arc-seedance-ip-licensing-ai-video/)

ByteDance's Volcano Arc platform addresses the IP problem in AI video generation by licensing real actor likenesses and film sets for revenue sharing. This approach sets a new standard for consent and compensation in AI video tools. ByteDance's Seedance model draws on this licensed content pipeline.
