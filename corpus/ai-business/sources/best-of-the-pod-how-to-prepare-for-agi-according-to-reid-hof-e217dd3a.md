# "🎧 Best of the Pod: How to Prepare for AGI According to Reid Hoffman"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://every.to/podcast/how-to-prepare-for-agi-according-to-reid-hoffman-96911938-43f0-4f4b-a4fe-fa89f1c51918)

Reid Hoffman discusses his book Superagency, exploring how humans can master AI and increase their agency. He shares insights on the psychological patterns of adopting new technologies and the importance of equitable access to AI. Hoffman emphasizes the need to treat uncertainty as a feature, not a bug, and to leverage AI to make better decisions.

- AI adoption
- Human agency
- Equitable access to AI
