## Vibe Coding

Ben Thompson reflects positively on vibe coding as a personal experience, noting that "technology, for all of its importance, is also fun."[^src] He describes using AI to build an app he plans to use regularly, and Stratechery published a multi-part series: *My Vibe Coding Adventure*, *The App and the Experience*, and *Ten Takeaways*.[^src]

The Midsummer Mailbag episode of Sharp Tech touched on vibe coding further alongside questions on memory chips, Sam Altman's PR strategy, and data centers in the ocean.[^src]

## Apple in Europe and Siri AI

Apple Intelligence products — branded as Siri AI — will not be released in Europe due to the company's ongoing dispute with European regulators over the Digital Markets Act (DMA).[^src] Ben Thompson's analysis argued that Apple's own policies "may well be what creates the long-term competitive changes the EU hopes to see."[^src] This was also discussed on Dithering with John Gruber.[^src]

Stratechery also covered Apple raising prices alongside the EU/Intelligence situation in the article *Apple Price Increases, Apple Intelligence and the E.U.*[^src]
