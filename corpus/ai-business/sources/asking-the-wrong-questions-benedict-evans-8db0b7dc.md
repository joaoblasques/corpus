# Asking the wrong questions — Benedict Evans

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.ben-evans.com/benedictevans/2017/01/11/wrongquestions)

The article discusses how past predictions about technological advancements often focus on the wrong aspects, such as specific features rather than the underlying platforms that enable them. It highlights the importance of understanding the structural impetus of computing and software in shaping technological progress. The author reflects on how this tendency to ask the wrong questions can lead to missed opportunities and incorrect assumptions.

**Key topics**
- Technological predictions
- Computing and software platforms
- Structural impetus of technological progress
- Predicting the wrong things
- Historical examples of technological advancements
