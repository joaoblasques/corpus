# Geopolitics - US National Security Strategy 2026

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-study-notes-geopolitics-us-national-security-strategy-2026.md`

The US National Security Strategy 2026 report outlines a shift towards economic nationalism, prioritizing domestic manufacturing, energy independence, and strategic trade relationships. This shift has implications for investors and businesses, particularly in the supply chain, defense, and energy sectors. The report identifies AI and semiconductors as core national security assets.

- US National Security Strategy 2026
- Economic Nationalism
- Supply Chain Reshoring
- AI and Semiconductors
- Energy Independence
- Trade Relationships
