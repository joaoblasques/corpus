# AI raises everyone's app quality but customers now vibecode their own clones to skip paying — @levelsio (Pieter Levels)

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://levels.io/ai-removes-app-layer-as-customers-vibecode-clones)

AI raises app quality but also increases competition and barrier to entry, leading to customers cloning apps to avoid paying. This changes the business chain from customer > app > AI model provider to customer > AI model provider, potentially removing the app layer.

**Key topics**
- AI-powered app quality
- Increased competition
- App cloning
- Barrier to entry
- AI model providers
- Personal software
