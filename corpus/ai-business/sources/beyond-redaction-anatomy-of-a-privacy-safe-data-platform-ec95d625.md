# "Beyond Redaction: Anatomy of a Privacy-Safe Data Platform"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.dataengineeringweekly.com/p/beyond-redaction-anatomy-of-a-privacy)

The article discusses the importance of privacy engineering in data platforms, focusing on the need to govern data in use rather than simply hiding it. It introduces a five-stage pipeline for privacy-safe data platforms and highlights the importance of control selection, authorization, and evidence-based decision-making.
