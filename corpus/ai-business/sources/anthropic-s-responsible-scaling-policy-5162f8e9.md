# Anthropic’s Responsible Scaling Policy

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.anthropic.com/responsible-scaling-policy)

Anthropic's Responsible Scaling Policy outlines the company's approach to ensuring the safe development and deployment of AI models. The policy emphasizes the need for proportional, iterative, and exportable risk governance. It also describes the company's plans for implementing ASL-3 safeguards to mitigate catastrophic risks associated with advanced models.
