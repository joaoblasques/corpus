# "Dear AI Companies: Stop the “Doom Trolling” - Cal Newport"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://calnewport.com/dear-ai-companies-stop-the-doom-trolling/)

The author criticizes AI companies for 'doom trolling', a style of publicity that creates unnecessary anxiety about AI's potential harm. They argue that this approach is hypocritical and damaging to mental health. The author calls for AI companies to be transparent and honest about their products.
