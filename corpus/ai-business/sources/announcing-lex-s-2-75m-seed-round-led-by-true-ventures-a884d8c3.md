# "Announcing Lex’s $2.75m Seed Round, Led by True Ventures"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://every.to/on-every/announcing-lex-s-2-75m-seed-round-led-by-true-ventures)

Lex, an AI-powered word processor, has spun out from Every and raised $2.75m in seed funding led by True Ventures. The company aims to help people form better ideas and communicate them in more compelling ways. Lex's CEO, Nathan, will lead the company as it continues to develop its product.
