# "Anthropic Economic Index report: Cadences"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.anthropic.com/research/economic-index-june-2026-report)

The report presents findings from the Anthropic Economic Index, which studies the economic impacts of AI. It highlights how AI usage mirrors work patterns, with personal prompts spiking on weekends. The report also explores the types of outputs produced by AI and their value, as well as user perceptions of AI's impact on work.

**Key topics**
- Anthropic Economic Index
- AI usage patterns
- Output types and value
- User perceptions of AI's impact
