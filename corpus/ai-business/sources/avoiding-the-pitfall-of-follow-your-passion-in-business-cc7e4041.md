# Avoiding the Pitfall of 'Follow Your Passion' in Business

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://thebootstrappedfounder.com/follow-your-passion-but-not-like-that/)

The article discusses the pitfalls of following one's passion in business, arguing that it can lead to unmonetizable skills and a lack of scalability. Instead, the author suggests finding a problem to solve for others who share the same passion, and building a business around that solution. This approach allows entrepreneurs to monetize their skills and stay connected to their passion.
