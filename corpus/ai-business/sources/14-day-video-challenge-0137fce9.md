# 14-Day Video Challenge

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.smartpassiveincome.com/14-day-video-challenge/)

The 14-Day Video Challenge is a free online course that helps creators build a loyal audience by publishing short-form videos daily for 14 days. It provides a simple system to follow and covers topics such as choosing a series format, crafting a hook, and creating engaging content. The challenge aims to help creators build confidence and momentum in their content creation journey.

**Key topics**
- Short-form video creation
- Content creation strategy
- Audience building
- Consistency in content creation
- Creator mindset and confidence
