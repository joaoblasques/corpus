# AI and Liability

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Jun/25/ai-and-liability/#atom-everything)

A German ruling holds Google liable for errors in AI overviews, treating AI agents as company representatives. This decision discourages companies from using AI to avoid accountability. Human writers, lawyers, and doctors would be preferred over cheaper AI alternatives.
