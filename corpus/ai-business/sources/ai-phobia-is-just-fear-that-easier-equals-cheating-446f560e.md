# AI Phobia Is Just Fear That ‘Easier’ Equals ‘Cheating’

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://every.to/working-overtime/ai-phobia-is-really-just-fear-that-easier-equals-cheating)

The article discusses how the fear of AI usage in writing is rooted in a cultural obsession with hard work and visible effort. It argues that this mindset is outdated and that AI can actually set people free by shifting the focus from effort to outcomes. The article also explores the history of moral anxiety about ease and how it has led to a dysfunctional relationship with work.
