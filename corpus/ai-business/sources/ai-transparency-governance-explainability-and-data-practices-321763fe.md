# "AI Transparency: Governance, Explainability, and Data Practices"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.databricks.com/blog/ai-transparency)

AI transparency is crucial for building trustworthy AI systems, ensuring compliance with regulations, and reducing bias. It involves documenting and disclosing how AI systems work, including training data, model documentation, and governance oversight. Transparency is essential for stakeholders to evaluate and trust AI outputs.

**Key topics**
- AI transparency
- explainability
- interpretability
- AI governance
- EU AI Act
- bias reduction
