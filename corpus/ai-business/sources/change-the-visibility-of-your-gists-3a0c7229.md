# Change the visibility of your Gists

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://github.blog/news-insights/product-news/change-the-visibility-of-your-gists/)

GitHub introduces a feature to change the visibility of Gists, allowing users to toggle between public and secret visibility. This feature aims to prevent accidental sharing of sensitive information. The change is part of GitHub's efforts to improve user experience and security.
