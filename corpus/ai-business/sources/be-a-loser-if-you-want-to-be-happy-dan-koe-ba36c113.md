# Be A Loser – If You Want To Be Happy - Dan Koe

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://thedankoe.com/letters/be-a-loser-if-you-want-to-be-happy/)

The article discusses the concept of success and happiness, arguing that they are not the same and that true success comes from being useful and making a positive impact. It proposes a new definition of success that combines progress, contribution, and emotional management. The author emphasizes the importance of self-mastery, true education, modern belonging, and contributing to society.
