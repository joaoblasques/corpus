# "AI Model Export Controls Explained: What Government Review Means for Your AI Stack"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.mindstudio.ai/blog/ai-model-export-controls-government-review-explained/)

The US government is reviewing frontier AI models before release, treating them as controlled technology similar to advanced semiconductors. This regulatory trend affects enterprise builders who depend on models like Claude, GPT, and Gemini. The key takeaway is that model availability risk is real, and compliance obligations for end users are emerging.
