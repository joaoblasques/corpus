# A Response to Our Reader Survey

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.dataengineeringweekly.com/p/a-response-to-our-reader-survey)

Data Engineering Weekly adjusts its editorial scope to focus on core data engineering topics, context engineering, and adjacent but relevant AI/ML topics. The newsletter will eliminate AI-adjacent content lacking substantial data-infrastructure center of gravity. A stricter standard for article evaluation is introduced.

**Key topics**
- Data Engineering Weekly
- Editorial Scope
- Context Engineering
- Adjacent but Relevant AI/ML Topics
- Vendor Neutrality
- Transparency Reports
