# "Data Architecture Comparison: Warehouse, Lake, Lakehouse, and Mesh"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-data-architecture-warehouse-lake-lakehouse-mesh-comparison.md`

This document provides a deep-dive comparison of four major data architectures: Data Warehouse, Data Lake, Data Lakehouse, and Data Mesh. It covers their trade-offs in cost, governance, complexity, and team structure. The key takeaway is to choose the right architecture based on data types, team structure, query patterns, governance needs, and scale.
