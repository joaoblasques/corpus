# Data Management Principles for Resilient Systems

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-data-management-principles-resilient-systems.md`

This document discusses data management principles for resilient systems, focusing on how systems connect and behave under stress. It presents five principles to shift the focus from components to integrations and from governance as constraint to governance as accelerant. True resilience is about how systems talk to each other and whether teams are empowered to act when things go sideways.

**Key topics**
- Resilience in systems
- Data management principles
- System integrations
- Governance as accelerant
- Stress-tested operational reality
- Decision-capable systems
