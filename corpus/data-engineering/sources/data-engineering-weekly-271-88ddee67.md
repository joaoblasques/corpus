# "Data Engineering Weekly #271"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.dataengineeringweekly.com/p/data-engineering-weekly-271)

This newsletter covers various data engineering topics, including building a data platform, data quality, and observability. It also features case studies from companies like Netflix, Grab, and Uber on their data engineering challenges and solutions. The key takeaway is the importance of composable architectures and data quality in data engineering.

- Composable Architectures

- Observability
