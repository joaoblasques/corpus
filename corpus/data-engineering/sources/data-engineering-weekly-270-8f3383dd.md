# "Data Engineering Weekly #270"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.dataengineeringweekly.com/p/data-engineering-weekly-270)

This newsletter covers various data engineering topics, including data platform fundamentals, data mesh, and operational challenges of enforcing ACLs. It also discusses AI agents in data engineering, data ingestion systems, and convergence of open table formats and open catalogs. Key takeaways include the importance of data quality, observability, and the use of AI agents in data engineering.

- Data Projects: Managing Data Assets at Netflix Scale

- Migrating Data Ingestion Systems at Meta Scale
- The Convergence of Open Table Formats and Open Catalogs: Catalog Commits is Generally Available
