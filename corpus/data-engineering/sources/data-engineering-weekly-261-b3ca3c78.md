# "Data Engineering Weekly #261"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.dataengineeringweekly.com/p/data-engineering-weekly-261)

This newsletter covers various data engineering topics, including orchestration of Databricks across multiple workspaces, the intersection of data architecture and AI, and the use of AI agents in data engineering. It also discusses the challenges of building efficient sandbox environments for brownfield projects and the importance of context-aware agent architectures. The newsletter highlights several real-world examples of data engineering in practice.

**Key topics**
- Databricks orchestration
- Data architecture and AI
- AI agents in data engineering
- Sandbox environments for brownfield projects
- Context-aware agent architectures
- Data engineering in practice
