# Data Modeling Grain — Getting Granularity Right to Prevent Silent Errors

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-data-modeling-grain-granularity-correctness.md`

Data modeling grain refers to the fundamental level of detail in a row, and getting it wrong can lead to silent errors. Incompatible grains and fan-out are common pitfalls to avoid. Proper grain documentation is crucial to prevent invisible errors.
