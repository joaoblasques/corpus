# AI Tools in Data Engineering — Where to Delegate and What to Own

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-ai-tools-data-engineering-fundamentals-ownership-boundaries.md`

The article discusses the risks of using AI coding tools in data engineering, specifically the risk of comprehension debt, where engineers rely on AI to build systems they cannot reason about. It highlights the distinction between work that is safe to delegate to AI and judgment-intensive decisions that engineers must own. The article provides practical workflows and guidelines for using AI tools effectively.
