# data-quality-monitoring-at-scale-with-agentic-ai

> **Quick intake** (web). [open source]("https://www.databricks.com/blog/data-quality-monitoring-scale-agentic-ai")

Databricks announces the Public Preview of Data Quality Monitoring on AWS, Azure Databricks, and GCP, replacing manual checks with an agentic approach built for scale. AI agents learn normal data patterns, adapt to change, and monitor the data estate continuously. This approach enables teams to detect issues earlier, focus on what matters most, and resolve problems faster at enterprise scale.
