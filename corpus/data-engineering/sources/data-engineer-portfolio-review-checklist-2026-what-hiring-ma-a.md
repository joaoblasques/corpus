# data-engineer-portfolio-review-checklist-2026-what-hiring-ma

> **Quick intake** (web). [open source]("https://dataengineeracademy.com/blog/data-engineer-portfolio-review-checklist-2026-what-hiring-managers-actually-score/")

A 2026-ready data engineering portfolio showcases real-world projects using modern tools, clear documentation, and an emphasis on solving actual business problems. Quality beats quantity, and hiring managers care less about fancy technologies than how they're applied. A well-crafted portfolio can be the deciding factor for a hiring manager choosing who gets the offer.
