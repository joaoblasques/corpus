# Data Engineering After AI

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.dataengineeringweekly.com/p/data-engineering-after-ai)

The article discusses the evolution of data engineering in the context of AI and the emergence of a new framework called ECL (Extract, Contextualize, Link). ECL replaces the traditional ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) approach and emphasizes the importance of giving data semantic meaning through contextualization. The article highlights the limitations of early binding contracts and introduces the concept of late binding through the Contextualize pipeline.

**Key topics**
- ECL framework
- Data engineering evolution
- Contextualization
- Early binding contracts
- Late binding
- Contextualize pipeline
