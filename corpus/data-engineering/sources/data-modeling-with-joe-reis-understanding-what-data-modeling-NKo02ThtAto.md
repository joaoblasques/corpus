# Data Modeling With Joe Reis - Understanding What Data Modeling Is And Where It's Going

The video discusses data modeling, its definition, and its importance in data engineering. Joe Reis defines data modeling as organizing and standardizing data to facilitate believable and useful information and knowledge for humans and machines. He emphasizes the need for a holistic view of data across its lifecycle and use cases.
