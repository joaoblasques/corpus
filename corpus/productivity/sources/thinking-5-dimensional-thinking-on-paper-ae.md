> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-study-notes-thinking-5-dimensional-thinking-on-paper.md`

The document discusses the benefits of writing down thoughts on paper, specifically using 5-dimensional thinking to externalize and process information.
