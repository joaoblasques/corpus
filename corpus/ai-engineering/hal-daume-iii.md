# Hal Daumé III

ML researcher and professor; author of *A Course in Machine Learning* (2015, open access at hal3.name/courseml/). Known for work in natural language processing and machine learning pedagogy. The textbook is pedagogically sequenced rather than topically organized, aimed at undergraduate and early graduate students. [^ciml-p01]
