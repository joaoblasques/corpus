# An off switch for dual use knowledge in AI models

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.anthropic.com/research/off-switch-dual-use)

Researchers propose a method called GRAM to control dual-use knowledge in AI models by adding removable compartments for each category of knowledge, allowing for more robust protection against misuse.
