# Google NotebookLM - AI Research Assistant

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-google-notebooklm-ai-research-assistant.md`

Google NotebookLM is a source-grounded AI research assistant built by Google, powered by Gemini. It provides responses backed by citations from uploaded documents, reducing hallucinations compared to general-purpose chatbots. The tool offers multi-modal outputs and a robust free tier.

- Google NotebookLM
- Source-grounded AI research assistant
- Gemini
- Retrieval Augmented Generation
- Multi-modal outputs
