# A global workspace in language models

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.anthropic.com/research/global-workspace)

Researchers discovered a 'J-space' in a language model, a mental workspace that enables deliberate reasoning and higher-order thinking. This workspace is separate from the model's automatic processing and allows it to think about concepts without writing them down. The J-space is a key component of the model's internal neural network.
