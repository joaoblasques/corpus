# "AI Integration Beyond Chatbots: Incremental Approach with SLMs"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-ai-integration-beyond-chatbots-slm-approach.md`

The article critiques the default 'build a chatbot' approach to AI integration, arguing for a more incremental strategy using Small Language Models (SLMs). It highlights the importance of proper context engineering and data readiness. The key takeaway is to start with internal product improvements before exposing AI to users.
