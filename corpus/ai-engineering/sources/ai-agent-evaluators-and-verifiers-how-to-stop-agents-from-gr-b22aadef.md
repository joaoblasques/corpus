# "AI Agent Evaluators and Verifiers: How to Stop Agents from Grading Their Own Work"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.mindstudio.ai/blog/ai-agent-evaluators-verifiers-stop-agents-grading-own-work-2/)

The article discusses the issue of AI agents evaluating their own work, which leads to biased results. It proposes the use of separate evaluator and verifier components to catch errors before they ship. The evaluator assesses the quality of the output against a defined rubric, while the verifier checks for correctness against ground truth.
