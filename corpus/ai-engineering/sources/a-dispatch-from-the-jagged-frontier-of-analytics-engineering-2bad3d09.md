# A Dispatch from the Jagged Frontier of Analytics Engineering

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://roundup.getdbt.com/p/a-dispatch-from-the-jagged-frontier)

The article discusses the current state of analytics engineering with the help of Large Language Models (LLMs). It highlights the strengths and weaknesses of using LLMs in analytics engineering tasks, including sessionization, data profiling, and schema exploration. The key takeaway is that while LLMs can significantly speed up analytics engineering work, they still struggle with tasks that require company-specific knowledge, judgment calls, and reconciling data across sources.

**Key topics**
- Analytics Engineering
- Large Language Models
- Sessionization
- Data Profiling
- Schema Exploration
- LLM Limitations
