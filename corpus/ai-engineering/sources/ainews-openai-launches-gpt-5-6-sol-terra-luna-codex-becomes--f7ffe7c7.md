OpenAI launches GPT-5.6 Sol/Terra/Luna, a new frontier model with improved performance and efficiency, and introduces ChatGPT Work, a new desktop app that merges Codex and GPT-5.6. The model is capable of executing complex tasks and has been shown to outperform previous models on various benchmarks.
