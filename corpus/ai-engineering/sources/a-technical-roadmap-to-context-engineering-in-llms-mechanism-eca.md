# a-technical-roadmap-to-context-engineering-in-llms-mechanism

> **Quick intake** (web). [open source]("https://www.marktechpost.com/2025/08/03/a-technical-roadmap-to-context-engineering-in-llms-mechanisms-benchmarks-and-open-challenges/")

Context Engineering is a discipline that optimizes information fed into Large Language Models (LLMs) for better performance. It involves organizing, assembling, and optimizing context to maximize comprehension, reasoning, and adaptability. The discipline has various components, including context retrieval, processing, and management.

**Key topics**
- Context Engineering
- Large Language Models (LLMs)
- Context Retrieval and Generation
- Context Processing
- Context Management
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
