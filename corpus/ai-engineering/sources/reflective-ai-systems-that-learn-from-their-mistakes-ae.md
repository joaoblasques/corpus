# Reflective AI - Systems That Learn From Their Mistakes

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-reflective-ai-systems-that-learn-from-their-mistakes.md`

Reflective AI refers to systems that can evaluate and improve their outputs. This capability is essential for advanced AI systems, improving accuracy without requiring additional training data. It's foundational to the next generation of autonomous AI agents.
