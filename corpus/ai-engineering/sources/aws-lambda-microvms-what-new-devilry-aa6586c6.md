# AWS Lambda MicroVMs - What new devilry

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://dataengineeringcentral.substack.com/p/aws-lambda-microvms-what-new-devilry)

AWS Lambda MicroVMs are a new serverless compute service that combines the benefits of EC2 and Lambda, offering isolated, persistent, and serverless virtual machines for AI applications and other use cases.
