# Neurosymbolic AI - Improving AI Reasoning

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-neurosymbolic-ai-improving-ai-reasoning.md`

Neurosymbolic AI combines neural networks with symbolic reasoning to improve AI systems' reasoning and decision-making capabilities. It aims to bridge the gap between pattern recognition and formal reasoning. This approach is gaining attention for its potential to create trustworthy and explainable AI.

**Key topics**
- Neurosymbolic AI
- Neural Networks
- Symbolic Reasoning
- Large Language Models
- Pattern Recognition
- Formal Reasoning
