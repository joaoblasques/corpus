# designing-dynamic-agentic-workflows-from-static-pipelines-to

Designing dynamic agentic workflows for AI systems involves breaking tasks into steps at runtime, allowing the system to decide how to execute them. This approach helps to improve reliability and scalability. The key takeaway is to focus on clear separation of concerns, controlled execution, and proper evaluation.
