# 2026 State of Analytics Engineering Report | dbt Labs

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.getdbt.com/resources/state-of-analytics-engineering-2026)

The 2026 State of Analytics Engineering Report highlights the growing importance of AI in analytics engineering, with 72% of respondents prioritizing AI-assisted coding. However, the report also notes that investment in validation, testing, and governance mechanisms is not scaling at the same rate, creating a risk of acceleration without stabilization. The report emphasizes the need for discipline in modeling, validation, and ownership to ensure reliable output.

**Key topics**
- AI-assisted coding
- Analytics engineering
- Trust and governance
- Acceleration without stabilization
- Data quality and ownership
- Infrastructure costs
