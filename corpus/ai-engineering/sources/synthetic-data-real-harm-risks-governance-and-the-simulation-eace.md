# "Synthetic Data, Real Harm — Risks, Governance, and the Simulation-to-Reality Gap"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-synthetic-data-real-harm-risks-governance.md`

The article discusses the risks and limitations of synthetic data in machine learning, particularly its potential to concentrate subjective design choices and introduce new risks. It argues that governance frameworks have not caught up with the use of synthetic data. The key takeaway is that synthetic data should be used with caution and only for specific use cases.
