# "Agent Skills: Disseminating Expertise"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://roundup.getdbt.com/p/agent-skills-disseminating-expertise)

The author discusses the concept of agent skills, which are bundles of prompts and procedural guidance that AI agents can load dynamically to perform tasks. They highlight the benefits of skills, including reduced friction and the ability to encode expertise in a more declarative way. The author also touches on the future of skills, including the potential for native package management and the role of skills in disseminating best practices knowledge.
