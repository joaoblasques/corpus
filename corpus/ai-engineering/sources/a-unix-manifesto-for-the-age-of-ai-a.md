# A Unix Manifesto for the Age of AI

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://linuxtoaster.com/manifesto.html)

The document discusses the importance of simplicity in AI software development, arguing that complexity is the default and simplicity requires a deliberate decision. It highlights the risks of AI accelerating complexity and the need for tools that promote simplicity and composable architecture. The author advocates for a Unix-like approach to AI development, where intelligence flows through pipes rather than accumulating in platforms.
