# AI agents and the data lake (w/ Lauren Anderson)

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://roundup.getdbt.com/p/ai-agents-and-the-data-lake-w-lauren)

Lauren Anderson discusses AI agents and the data lake, highlighting the importance of central governance and a shared semantic layer. She shares her experience in data engineering and analytics, and provides insights on how to manage identity and access for AI agents. The key takeaway is the need for a central governance plane to ensure safe and ethical AI.

**Key topics**
- AI agents
- Data lake
- Central governance
- Semantic layer
- Identity management
- Agent access patterns
