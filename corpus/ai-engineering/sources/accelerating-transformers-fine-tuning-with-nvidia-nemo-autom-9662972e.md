# Accelerating Transformers Fine-Tuning with NVIDIA NeMo AutoModel

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://huggingface.co/blog/nvidia/accelerating-fine-tuning-nvidia-nemo-automodel)

NVIDIA NeMo AutoModel is a library for building custom generative AI models at scale, providing a 3.4-3.7x speedup over Transformers v5 with 29-32% less GPU memory. It leverages Expert Parallelism, DeepEP, and TransformerEngine kernels for efficient training. The library is compatible with HuggingFace Transformers and enables open-source community contributions.

**Key topics**
- NVIDIA NeMo AutoModel
- Expert Parallelism
- DeepEP
- TransformerEngine kernels
- MoE models
- Transformers v5
