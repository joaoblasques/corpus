# "Agentic Engineering vs Vibe Coding: Google's Spectrum and What It Means for Your Stack"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.mindstudio.ai/blog/agentic-engineering-vs-vibe-coding-google-spectrum-2/)

Google's AI coding spectrum defines a progression from vibe coding to agentic engineering, with each stage requiring more deliberate design but producing more reliable output. Vibe coding is suitable for low-stakes, fast-iteration work, while agentic engineering is necessary for production systems, customer-facing products, and autonomous workflows. The framework emphasizes the importance of context management, task decomposition, feedback, and human control points in building reliable AI-powered systems.
