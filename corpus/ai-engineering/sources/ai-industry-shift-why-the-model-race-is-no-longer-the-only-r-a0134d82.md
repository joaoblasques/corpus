# "AI Industry Shift: Why the Model Race Is No Longer the Only Race That Matters"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.mindstudio.ai/blog/ai-industry-shift-model-race-no-longer-only-race/)

The AI model race is shifting from raw capability to infrastructure, distribution, and application depth, with Meta and Google focusing on commoditizing the model layer and capturing value through compute infrastructure and distribution scale respectively.

**Key topics**
- Meta Llama strategy
- OpenAI Stargate commitment
- Google distribution advantage
- AI model commoditization
- Application layer value accumulation
