# AI Observability - Monitoring AI in Production

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-ai-observability-monitoring-ai-in-production.md`

AI Observability refers to monitoring AI systems in production, tracking model behavior, debugging failures, and understanding how outputs are generated. It addresses problems unique to AI, such as hallucination, drift, and non-determinism. A new ecosystem of specialized tooling is emerging around this need.

**Key topics**
- AI Observability
- Model Behavior
- Debugging Failures
- Arize AI
- Langfuse
- Weights & Biases
