# "AI in ERP: Transforming Development and Maintenance"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://zencoder.ai/blog/how-ai-is-transforming-erp-development-and-maintenance)

The integration of AI in ERP development is transforming business operations by enhancing efficiency, intelligence, and user experience. AI accelerates development cycles, reduces manual effort, and optimizes system performance. It also enables predictive maintenance, automated updates, and proactive issue detection.

- AI in ERP development
- ERP system design
- AI-powered customization and configuration
- Predictive maintenance
- Automated updates and upgrades
