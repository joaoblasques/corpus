# Physical AI - When AI Gets a Body

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-physical-ai-when-ai-gets-a-body.md`

Physical AI refers to AI systems embedded in machines that can sense, move, and interact with the real world. This concept combines AI models, robotics, and sensors to enable AI to interact with the physical environment. Simulation is key to training physical AI safely.

**Key topics**
- Physical AI
- AI models + robotics + sensors
- Simulation environments
- Edge AI
- Synthetic Data
- Deep Reinforcement Learning
