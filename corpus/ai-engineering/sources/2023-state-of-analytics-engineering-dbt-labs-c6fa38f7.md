# 2023 State of Analytics Engineering | dbt Labs

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.getdbt.com/resources/state-of-analytics-engineering-2023)

The 2023 State of Analytics Engineering report by dbt Labs highlights trends, challenges, and best practices in the field of analytics engineering. The report is based on a survey of 567 data practitioners worldwide and covers topics such as data team productivity, data ownership, and investment in data quality and observability. Key takeaways include the importance of clear goals, valued work, and stakeholder needs.

**Key topics**
- Analytics Engineering
- Data Team Productivity
- Data Ownership
- Investment in Data Quality and Observability
- Clear Goals
- Valued Work
