# "AI Industry Shift: Why the Model Race Is No Longer the Only Race That Matters"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.mindstudio.ai/blog/ai-industry-shift-model-race-infrastructure-regulation/)

The AI industry has shifted from a model performance race to a focus on infrastructure, regulation, and distribution. Model performance is converging, and the real competition is in agent orchestration, compliance, and vendor stability. Enterprise AI buyers prioritize security, auditability, and reliability over model quality.

**Key topics**
- AI model performance convergence
- Agent orchestration
- Compliance in AI
- Enterprise AI buying trends
- Model selection for enterprise AI
- AI regulation
