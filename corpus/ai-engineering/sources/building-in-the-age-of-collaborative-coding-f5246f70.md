# Building in the Age of Collaborative Coding

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.builder.io/blog/building-in-the-age-of-collaborative-coding)

The article discusses the shift in collaborative coding models with AI tools, focusing on how to adopt a new workflow that integrates AI agents into shared workflows. It highlights five patterns for this new workflow, including instant code generation, direct collaboration with agents, and moving validation earlier in the process. The key takeaway is that AI is an invitation to redesign the workflow from scratch.
