# How spec-driven development improves AI coding quality | Red Hat Developer

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-how-spec-driven-development-improves-ai-coding-quality.md`

Spec-driven development improves AI coding quality by providing structure and precision, reducing errors and increasing maintainability. This approach involves creating detailed specifications before coding, engaging stakeholders early, and using iterative review processes. Effective specifications are crucial for successful AI coding.
