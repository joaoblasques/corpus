# AI Has Learned How to Capture Our Emotional Metadata

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://every.to/napkin-math/the-age-of-vibe-compute)

The article discusses the emergence of 'vibe compute', where AI models can read between the lines of a prompt and pick up on the intent behind it, rather than just executing tasks. This shift is made possible by technical breakthroughs that enable LLMs to encode subtle emotional context. The article explores the implications of this shift and its potential applications.
