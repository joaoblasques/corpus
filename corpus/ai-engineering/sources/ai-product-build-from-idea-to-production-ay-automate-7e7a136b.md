# "AI Product Build: From Idea to Production | AY Automate"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.ayautomate.com/workflows/ai-product-build)

A workflow for building and deploying AI products, focusing on front-loading risk assessment and measurement to ensure successful production. The process involves prototyping the AI core, evaluating its quality and cost, and then building a reliable shell around it. This approach helps prevent AI products from failing between demo and production.

**Key topics**
- AI Product Build
- Risk Assessment
- Measurement
- Prototype
- Eval Harness
- Production Shell
