# "A First Comprehensive Study of TurboQuant: Accuracy and Performance"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://vllm.ai/blog/2026-05-11-turboquant)

A comprehensive study evaluates the accuracy and performance of TurboQuant, a method for KV-cache quantization, and compares it to FP8 KV-cache quantization. The study finds that FP8 remains the best default for KV-cache quantization, providing 2x capacity with negligible accuracy loss and sometimes improved performance. TurboQuant variants show varying degrees of accuracy degradation and performance impact.

**Key topics**
- TurboQuant
- KV-cache quantization
- FP8
- accuracy and performance evaluation
- KV-cache capacity
- GPU memory savings
