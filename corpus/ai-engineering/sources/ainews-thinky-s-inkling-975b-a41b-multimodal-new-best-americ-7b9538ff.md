# "[AINews] Thinky's Inkling: 975B-A41B multimodal, new best American Apache 2.0 open model (with Inkling-Small, 276B-A12B)"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.latent.space/p/ainews-thinkys-inkling-975b-a41b)

Thinking Machines Lab released Inkling, a 975B-parameter, multimodal, open-weights foundation model. It supports text, image, and audio inputs, with text output, and is available on Tinker, Hugging Face, and partners. Inkling is positioned as a customizable base model rather than a benchmark-maxed flagship.
