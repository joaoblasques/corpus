# "Claude Science, an AI workbench for scientists"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-science-ai-workbench)

Claude Science is an AI workbench for scientists that integrates tools and packages, produces auditable artifacts, and provides flexible access to computing resources. It helps researchers analyze literature, execute multi-step research, and produce detailed artifacts with an auditable history. The platform is designed to accelerate scientific discovery and development of healthcare interventions.
