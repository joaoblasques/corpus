# Stop using AGENTS.md and CLAUDE.md (do this instead)

> **Quick intake** (YouTube · ZazenCodes · playlist _Corpus_queue_). [watch on YouTube](https://youtu.be/SKEB0DxByqc) · [transcript](../../../raw/youtube/youtube-SKEB0DxByqc-stop-using-agents-md-and-claude-md-do-this-instead.md)

The video discusses the limitations of using AGENTS.md and CLAUDE.md files for coding agents, citing research that found they are not as helpful as previously thought. The speaker explains the research methodology and results, which tested three workflows: developer-provided instructions, no instructions, and coding agent-generated files. The key takeaway is that overly verbose context files may not be as effective as simpler, more focused ones.
