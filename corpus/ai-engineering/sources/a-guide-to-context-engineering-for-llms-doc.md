# a-guide-to-context-engineering-for-llms

> **Quick intake** (web). [open source]("https://blog.bytebytego.com/p/a-guide-to-context-engineering-for")

The article discusses the challenges of working with Large Language Models (LLMs) and introduces the concept of context engineering, which involves designing and managing the information environment an LLM sees before generating a response. It highlights the importance of context engineering in achieving better results with LLMs and presents four strategies for managing context: write, select, compress, and isolate. The article also touches on the trade-offs involved in these strategies and the need for a nuanced approach to context engineering.

- Context Engineering
- Large Language Models (LLMs)
- Attention Mechanism
- Context Window
- Statelessness
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
