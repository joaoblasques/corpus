# "AIEWF Daily Dispatch: Autoresearch and the tension between AI and human agency"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.latent.space/p/aiewf-daily-dispatch-agency)

The article discusses the tension between AI and human agency in the context of autoresearch, a loop that involves agents maintaining and studying the system. It highlights the importance of human understanding and control in the software development process. The key takeaway is that humans must still define goals, judge results, and take responsibility for what the loop produces.
