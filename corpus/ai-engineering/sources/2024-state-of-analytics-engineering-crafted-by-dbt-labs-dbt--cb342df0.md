# "2024 State of Analytics Engineering, crafted by dbt Labs | dbt Labs"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.getdbt.com/resources/state-of-analytics-engineering-2024)

The 2024 State of Analytics Engineering report highlights key trends in data quality, accessibility, and AI adoption among global data teams. It emphasizes the importance of data quality, decentralized data architectures, and AI in data management. The report also explores the challenges faced by data teams, including poor data quality, unclear data ownership, and stakeholder data literacy.

**Key topics**
- Data Quality
- Decentralized Data Architectures
- AI Adoption
- Data Management
- Data Teams
- Analytics Engineering
