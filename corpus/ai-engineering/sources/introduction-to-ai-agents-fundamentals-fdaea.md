# "Introduction to AI Agents: Fundamentals"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-ai-agents-introduction-fundamentals.md`

This document introduces AI agents as a paradigm shift from passive, discrete AI tasks to complete applications that combine reasoning and action. AI agents can autonomously plan and execute complex tasks without human direction. This represents a move from human-directed AI to goal-directed autonomous execution.

**Key topics**
- AI Agents
- Agentic AI
- Autonomous Problem-Solving
- Language Model Reasoning
- Goal-Directed Execution
