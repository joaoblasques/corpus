# "Data Bottlenecks in AI Projects: How Versioning Helps"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://lakefs.io/blog/data-bottlenecks-for-ai-projects/)

Data bottlenecks in AI projects can be caused by various factors such as slow ETL pipelines, inconsistent datasets, and poor reproducibility. Versioned data processes can help increase reproducibility, isolate experiments, and decrease operational overhead. Effective data management is crucial for faster iteration and production-ready MLOps operations.
