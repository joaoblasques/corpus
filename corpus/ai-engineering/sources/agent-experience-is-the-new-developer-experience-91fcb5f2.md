# Agent experience is the new developer experience

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.builder.io/blog/agent-experience)

The article discusses the importance of designing a feedback loop for AI agents, similar to the developer experience, to improve their productivity and accuracy. It highlights the need for a deterministic environment, minimal context, and transparent rules for agents. The author argues that good agent experience is crucial for teams to collaborate effectively with AI agents.
