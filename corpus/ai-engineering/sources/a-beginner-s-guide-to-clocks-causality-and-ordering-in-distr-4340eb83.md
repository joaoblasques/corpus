# "A Beginner’s Guide to Clocks, Causality, and Ordering in Distributed Systems"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://blog.bytebytego.com/p/a-beginners-guide-to-clocks-causality)

The article discusses the challenges of ordering events in distributed systems, where clocks on different machines can be out of sync, leading to incorrect results. It explores the concepts of logical clocks, vector clocks, and hybrid logical clocks, and how they can be used to determine the order of events. The article also touches on the importance of ordering in practice, particularly in replication, auditing, and debugging.

**Key topics**
- Clocks in Distributed Systems
- Causality and Ordering
- Logical Clocks
- Vector Clocks
- Hybrid Logical Clocks
- Distributed System Challenges
