# "Confidence-Scheduled Verification: How DeepSpark Cuts Wasted GPU Compute in AI Agents"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.mindstudio.ai/blog/deepspark-confidence-scheduled-verification-ai-agents/)

Confidence-scheduled verification is a technique that optimizes AI agent inference by selectively skipping verifier forward passes based on the draft model's confidence scores. This approach reduces GPU compute waste and improves throughput, especially under heavy load conditions. By dynamically adjusting verification thresholds, it maintains output quality while absorbing traffic spikes.
