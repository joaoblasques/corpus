# Ali Ghodsi | Databricks

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.databricks.com/dataaisummit/speaker/ali-ghodsi)

Ali Ghodsi is the CEO and co-founder of Databricks, responsible for its growth and international expansion. He is also an adjunct professor at UC Berkeley and a creator of Apache Spark. Ghodsi's academic research has been applied to Apache Mesos and Apache Hadoop.
