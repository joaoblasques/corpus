# "Context Rot in AI Agents: What It Is and How to Fix It with Session Handoffs"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.mindstudio.ai/blog/context-rot-ai-agents-session-handoff-fix/)

Context rot is a gradual degradation of AI model output quality due to a finite context window, leading to vague answers and contradictions. Session handoffs can fix this by summarizing key information and restarting the session. This approach is crucial for agents that handle long conversations and take actions.
