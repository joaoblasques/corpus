# OpenClaw-RL — Asynchronous Reinforcement Learning for Personalized AI Agents

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-openclaw-rl-async-reinforcement-learning-personal-agents.md`

OpenClaw-RL is a fully asynchronous reinforcement learning framework for training personalized AI agents through natural conversation feedback. It enables zero manual labeling and self-hosted deployment. The framework reached #1 on HuggingFace Daily Papers at launch.

**Key topics**
- OpenClaw-RL
- Asynchronous Reinforcement Learning
- Personalized AI Agents
- Natural Conversation Feedback
- Zero Manual Labeling
- Self-Hosted Deployment
