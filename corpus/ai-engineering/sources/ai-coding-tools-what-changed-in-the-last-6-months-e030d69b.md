# "AI Coding Tools: What Changed in the Last 6 Months"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://theaiengineer.substack.com/p/ai-coding-tools-what-changed-in-the)

The article discusses the recent changes in AI coding tools, including the shift from in-editor autocomplete to autonomous agents, the rise of model makers, and the adoption of usage-based billing. It highlights the challenges and limitations of these tools, including the need for review and verification of agent output. The key takeaway is that the editor you pick matters less, and the ability to review and verify agent output quickly is now the new ceiling on how fast you can ship.

- usage-based billing
