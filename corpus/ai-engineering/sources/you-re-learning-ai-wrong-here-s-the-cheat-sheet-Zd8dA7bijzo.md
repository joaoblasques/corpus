# You're Learning AI Wrong. Here's The Cheat Sheet.

> **Quick intake** (YouTube · AI Founders · playlist _AI Engineering_). [watch on YouTube](https://youtu.be/Zd8dA7bijzo) · [transcript](../../../raw/youtube/youtube-Zd8dA7bijzo-you-re-learning-ai-wrong-here-s-the-cheat-sheet.md)

The video discusses the importance of understanding the layers of AI systems to avoid misdiagnosis and build successful AI businesses. It introduces the 'periodic table of AI elements' to help entrepreneurs identify the relevant group of elements for their project. The key takeaway is that understanding the map of AI elements is more important than memorizing every detail.
