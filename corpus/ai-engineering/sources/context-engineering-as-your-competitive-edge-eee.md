# Context Engineering as Your Competitive Edge

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-context-engineering-competitive-edge-domain-expertise.md`

Context engineering is a competitive advantage when combined with unique domain expertise. It involves dynamically filling the context window with information that maximizes an AI model's success. The article examines three pillars of context: Knowledge, Tools, and Memory.
