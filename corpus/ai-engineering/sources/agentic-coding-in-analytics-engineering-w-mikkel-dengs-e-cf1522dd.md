# Agentic coding in analytics engineering (w/ Mikkel Dengsøe)

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://roundup.getdbt.com/p/agentic-coding-in-analytics-engineering)

Mikkel Dengsøe discusses his experiences with agentic coding tools in analytics engineering, covering data modeling, testing, and exposing data in Omni's AI agent. He highlights the benefits of using these tools, including increased productivity and improved testing. The article also touches on the future of observability, moving from dashboards to root-cause explanations delivered to the right person at the right time.

- Agentic coding
- Analytics engineering
- Data modeling
- Testing
- Omni's AI agent
- Observability
