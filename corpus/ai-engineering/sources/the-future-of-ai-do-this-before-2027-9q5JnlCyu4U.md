# The Future of AI (Do This Before 2027)

> **Quick intake** (YouTube · Grant Owen · playlist _Corpus_queue_). [watch on YouTube](https://youtu.be/9q5JnlCyu4U) · [transcript](../../../raw/youtube/youtube-9q5JnlCyu4U-the-future-of-ai-do-this-before-2027.md)

The video discusses the misconception that everyone is using AI, and how to identify the right problem to solve in AI adoption. It emphasizes the importance of understanding the bottleneck or constraint in a business or process before trying to solve it with technology. The key takeaway is to focus on the minimum effective dose to solve the problem and get out of one's own way.

- AI adoption
