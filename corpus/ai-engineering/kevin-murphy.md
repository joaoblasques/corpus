# Kevin P. Murphy

TL;DR: ML researcher at Google; author of three major probabilistic ML textbooks; won the De Groot prize (2013) for *Machine Learning: A Probabilistic Perspective* (2012). Known for the breadth and mathematical rigor of his treatments.

## Key contributions

- ***Machine Learning: A Probabilistic Perspective*** (MIT Press, 2012) — 1200-page comprehensive ML reference; won the De Groot prize in 2013. [^pml-p01]
- ***Probabilistic Machine Learning: An Introduction*** (MIT Press, 2022; CC-BY-NC-ND) — 860-page modernized first volume emphasizing deep learning, Python (JAX/PyTorch), and probabilistic framing. [^pml-p01]
- ***Probabilistic Machine Learning: Advanced Topics*** (MIT Press, 2023) — sequel covering generative models, variational inference, RL, and more.
- Helped develop the exposure notification risk score algorithm for Google's COVID-19 exposure notification app. [^pml-p01]
- Organized Google Summer of Code for PML book code contributions; involved in JAX/PyTorch educational ecosystem.
