# Roberto Battiti

ML/optimization researcher at LIONlab, University of Trento, Italy (alumnus of Caltech). Co-author of *The LION Way: Machine Learning plus Intelligent Optimization* (with Mauro Brunato, 2017). Creator of Reactive Search Optimization (RSO) — the framework for online learning applied to metaheuristic search. [^src1]
