# Christopher M. Bishop

TL;DR: British machine learning researcher; longtime Microsoft Research Cambridge; author of the canonical Bayesian ML textbook *Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning* (2006); Fellow of the Royal Academy of Engineering. Microsoft Technical Fellow (Distinguished Scientist level).

## Key contributions

- **PRML (2006)**: *Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning* (Springer) — the dominant graduate textbook for Bayesian machine learning for ~15 years. Unified treatment from probability theory through graphical models, variational inference, and sampling. [^prml-p01]
- Earlier textbook: *Neural Networks for Pattern Recognition* (Oxford, 1995a) — foundational text on neural-network theory.
- Research at Microsoft Research Cambridge: Bayesian approaches to neural networks, variational methods, graphical models.
- F.R.Eng. (Fellow of the Royal Academy of Engineering); Cambridge address: CB3 0FB.
