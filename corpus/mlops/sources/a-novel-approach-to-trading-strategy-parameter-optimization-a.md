# a-novel-approach-to-trading-strategy-parameter-optimization

> **Quick intake** (web). [open source]("https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10785")

A novel approach to trading strategy parameter optimization using double out-of-sample data and walk-forward techniques is introduced. The study demonstrates the importance of optimizing walk-forward window lengths and emphasizes the value of single-time out-of-sample testing for reliable strategy evaluation. The approach is shown to outperform a Buy-and-Hold strategy and achieve a 50 percent reduction in drawdown when combined with Buy-and-Hold.

**Key topics**
- Trading strategy parameter optimization
- Double out-of-sample data
- Walk-forward techniques
- Robust Sharpe Ratio
- Exponential Moving Average
- Bitcoin
