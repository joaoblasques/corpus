# "Code is the Canvas: Bring the Whole Team to It"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.builder.io/blog/code-is-the-canvas)

The article discusses the shift in software development workflows with the advent of AI, where code becomes the canvas for the whole team to work together. It highlights the limitations of traditional workflows and introduces Builder, a tool that enables teams to build software together in a more efficient and collaborative way. The key takeaway is that treating code as the starting point for the whole team can significantly improve productivity and reduce the gap between what teams want to ship and what they actually ship.
