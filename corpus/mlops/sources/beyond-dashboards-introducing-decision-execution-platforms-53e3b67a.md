# "Beyond dashboards: Introducing Decision Execution Platforms"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.databricks.com/blog/beyond-dashboards-introducing-decision-execution-platforms)

Databricks introduces Decision Execution Platforms (DEPs), a new category of enterprise analytics that enables business outcomes by automating executive decision-making. DEPs break down the decision-making process into four stages: signal, decision, execution, and outcome. This approach aims to move beyond traditional dashboards and analytics platforms.
