# "Ask, build, compose: What our 5th Genie Hackathon taught us about Databricks Genie"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://www.databricks.com/blog/ask-build-compose-what-our-5th-genie-hackathon-taught-us-about-databricks-genie)

The article discusses the Databricks Genie hackathon, where teams built projects using Genie Agents, Genie Code, and agentic Genie. The projects showcased the capabilities of Genie in automating data tasks, building governance platforms, and composing tools for data analysis. The key takeaway is that Genie enables users to talk to their data, build with it, and compose it into a unified experience.
