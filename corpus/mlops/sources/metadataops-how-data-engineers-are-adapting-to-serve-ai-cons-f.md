# MetadataOps — How Data Engineers Are Adapting to Serve AI Consumers

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-metadataops-data-engineers-ai-era-shift.md`

The article discusses the shift in data engineering, where AI systems have replaced humans as primary consumers of data pipelines. Metadata is now the primary interface between data assets and AI agents, forcing data engineers to adapt and master the role of MetadataOps Engineer. This transition requires a focus on metadata quality, AI-readiness testing, and data governance.
