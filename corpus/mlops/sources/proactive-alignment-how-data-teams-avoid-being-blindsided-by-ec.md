# Proactive Alignment — How Data Teams Avoid Being Blindsided by Surprise Projects

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-proactive-data-team-alignment-avoiding-surprise-projects.md`

Data teams can avoid being blindsided by surprise projects by engaging in proactive alignment with business priorities. This involves seeking information early enough to shape incoming work, rather than just absorbing it. A simple 10-minute conversation with the CPO or PM can make a significant difference.
