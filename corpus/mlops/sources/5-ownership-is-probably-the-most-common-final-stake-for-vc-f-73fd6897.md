# 5% ownership is probably the most common final stake for VC funded startup founders — @levelsio (Pieter Levels)

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://levels.io/founder-ownership-dilution-vc-startups)

The document discusses the common final stake of VC funded startup founders, which is around 5% ownership, and how it changes based on the number of co-founders. It also calculates the expected outcome for a startup that gets acquired, taking into account the median acquisition value and time of acquisition. The key takeaway is that founders may not retain a significant portion of their company's value.
