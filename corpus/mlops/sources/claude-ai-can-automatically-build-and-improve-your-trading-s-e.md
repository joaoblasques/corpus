# Claude AI Can Automatically Build and Improve Your Trading Systems

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-study-notes-claude-ai-automatically-builds-and-improves-trading-systems.md`

Claude AI is an automated trading system development tool that uses AI to iteratively build, test, and optimize trading strategies without manual coding. It uses a custom backtesting engine and TradingView for verification. The tool can save time and improve strategy development.
