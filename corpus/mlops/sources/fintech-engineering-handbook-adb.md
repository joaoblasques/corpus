# Fintech Engineering Handbook

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-clippings-fintech-engineering-handbook.md`

This handbook covers principles and best practices for fintech engineering, focusing on money representation, currency handling, and recording money movements in a ledger. It emphasizes the importance of no invented data, no lost data, and no trust. The key takeaway is that accurate and reliable financial systems require careful consideration of these principles.

**Key topics**
- Money Representation
- Currency Handling
- Double-Entry Bookkeeping
- Event Sourcing
- Immutability
- Audit Trails
