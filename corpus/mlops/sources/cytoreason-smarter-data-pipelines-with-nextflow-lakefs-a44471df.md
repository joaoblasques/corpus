> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://lakefs.io/blog/cytoreason-smarter-data-pipelines/)

CytoReason uses lakeFS and Nextflow to version and manage their data pipelines, resulting in faster, cleaner, and more reproducible models.
