# 6 Software Engineering Challenges & Ways To Overcome Them

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://zencoder.ai/blog/software-engineering-challenges)

This article discusses 6 common software engineering challenges and provides practical strategies to overcome them. It covers topics such as unrealistic deadlines, unclear requirements, poor communication, and maintaining code quality. The key takeaway is that by adopting agile methodologies, improving estimation, and fostering a culture of open communication, software teams can improve their development process and deliver better results.
