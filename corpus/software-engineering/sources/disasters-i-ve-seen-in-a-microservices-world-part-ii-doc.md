# "Disasters I've seen in a microservices world, part II"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-disasters-in-a-microservices-world-part-ii.md`

The article discusses common challenges and pitfalls in microservices architecture, including high service-to-engineer ratios, gateway complexities, technology sprawl, and organizational structure impacts. It provides practical examples and best practices for managing services, optimizing gateway design, and controlling technology sprawl. The key takeaway is to balance service management with team capacity and ensure architecture is adaptable to organizational changes.

**Key topics**
- Microservices Architecture
- Service Management
- Gateway Efficiency
- Technology Standardization
- Organizational Alignment
- Continuous Learning
