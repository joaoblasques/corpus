# Software Engineering in the Age of AI

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-clippings-software-engineering-in-the-age-of-ai.md`

The author discusses the impact of AI on software engineering, highlighting the shift from creative problem-solving to editing and reviewing AI-generated code. They argue that this shift can lead to a loss of skills and creativity, as well as a reliance on systems that people do not understand. The author also touches on the long-term consequences of offloading creative work to bots.

- AI-generated code
