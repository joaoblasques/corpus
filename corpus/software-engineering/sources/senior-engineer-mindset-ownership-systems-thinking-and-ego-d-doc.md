# "Senior Engineer Mindset — Ownership, Systems Thinking, and Ego Dissolution"

> **Quick intake** (obsidian). raw stub: `notes-03-resources-articles-senior-engineer-mindset-ownership-systems-thinking.md`

A conversation between Yordan Ivanov and Erfan Hesami discussing the key differences between senior and mid-level engineers. Senior engineers focus on optimizing the environment where code lives, rather than individual code. This involves systems-level awareness, humility, and a focus on business outcomes.

**Key topics**
- Senior Engineer Mindset
- Ownership
- Systems Thinking
- Ego Dissolution
- Mid-Level Trap
- Technical Complexity
