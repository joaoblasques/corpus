# 14 More lessons from 14 years at Google

> **Quick intake** (obsidian-list). [open source](https://open.substack.com/pub/addyo/p/14-more-lessons-from-14-years-at)

The article shares 14 lessons learned from 14 years at Google, focusing on team dynamics, trust, and systems around code. It emphasizes the importance of making it easier for normal people to do extraordinary things on a normal day. The key takeaway is that reliability, observability, and trust are crucial for successful engineering teams.
