Generate higher quality code from AI tools! Learn prompting techniques that work consistently across Claude, ChatGPT, Copilot, and Cursor. Transform vague project ideas into structure, actionable development plans. Stay productive by applying these future-proof prompting strategies as AI tools and models evolve.

Sabrina teaching her ( https://frontendmasters.com/courses/prompt-engineering/?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=prompteng )

*What you’ll learn:*

* Understand how temperature, top-p, and context windows shape LLM behavior and output quality.
* Apply zero-shot, one-shot, and few-shot prompting to guide models with increasing precision.
* Use structured outputs, chain-of-thought reasoning, and delimiters to improve consistency and readability.
* Experiment with model selection, emotional cues, and personas to tailor responses to your goals.
* Document and test prompts regularly to future-proof them as new AI models evolve.
