 How to get the most from Claude Fable 5 





 Give it the goal, not the steps 


 Describe the outcome and let Claude Fable 5 plan the path. Use it with /goal 
to keep Claude working until a completion condition is met.





 Size up asynchronous tasks and walk away 


 Hand it work you’d normally break up. It’s able to hold multi-hour and 
multi-day sessions without losing the thread.





 Skip the guardrail prompts 


 You don’t need to note things like “make sure to test.” Claude Fable 5 is 
built to review its own work with less prompting. Qualitative feedback works 
better than itemized instructions.





 Hand it ambiguous problems 


 Root-causing, outage debugging, architecture calls. Claude Fable 5 can more 
reliably identify underlying causes, instead of simply symptoms.

 How safeguards work 


 Claude Fable 5 shares the same underlying model as Claude Mythos 5, but with 
additional safeguards, particularly in the cyber and bio domains. These 
safeguards allow us to share this intelligence more broadly.


 When a request touches a high-risk area like cybersecurity or biology, Claude 
Fable 5 may route it to Claude Opus 4.8.





 Claude Fable 5 requires a limited retention period. Retained data is used 
only to detect and prevent serious misuse, and is not used to train Claude.
